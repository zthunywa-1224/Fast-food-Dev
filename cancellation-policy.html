Cancellation Policy

We understand plans change. You can cancel an order up to 30 minutes after it’s placed, and we’ll give you a full refund.

How to cancel

  1. Go to “My Orders” on our website or app.
  2. Select the order you wish to cancel and click “Cancel Order”.
  3. Confirm the cancellation – you’ll receive an email confirmation and a refund will be processed automatically.

What we can’t cancel

  • Orders that have already been prepared for delivery or are out for delivery.
  • Orders that have been partially delivered.

If you need to cancel outside the 30‑minute window, please contact us at support@fastfooddev.com and we’ll do our best to help.
