Refund Policy

We want you to enjoy every bite. If you’re not completely satisfied with your order, please let us know within 24 hours of delivery so we can make it right.

How refunds work

  • If the issue is with the food (wrong item, missing items, or poor quality), we’ll either replace the order or issue a full refund – whichever you prefer.
  • Refunds are processed to the original payment method and typically appear within 3‑5 business days.
  • Discounts or promotional codes used on the original purchase are not refundable as cash; they’ll be re‑applied to a future order if you choose a replacement.

When we don’t offer refunds

  • Orders that have been partially consumed or are no longer in their original condition.
  • Changes of mind after the 24‑hour window.
  • Delivery fees – these are non‑refundable unless the order itself is incorrect or missing.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact our support team. We’re here to help!
